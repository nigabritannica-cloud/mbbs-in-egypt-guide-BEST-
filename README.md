Study MBBS in Egypt – Complete Guide
Studying MBBS abroad has become a popular option for Indian students looking for affordable and globally recognized medical education. Egypt is one of the emerging destinations offering quality education, modern infrastructure, and strong clinical exposure.

Many universities in Egypt provide English-medium programs, making it easier for international students to adapt. The cost of studying MBBS in Egypt is also lower compared to private medical colleges in India.

Students must qualify NEET and meet eligibility criteria to apply. The universities are recognized by global medical bodies, allowing students to explore career opportunities worldwide.
